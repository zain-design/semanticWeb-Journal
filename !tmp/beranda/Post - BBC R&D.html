        Old School: An 8K Multicamera Shoot to Create a Dataset for Computational Cinematography

        Graeme Phillipson, Stephen Jolly, Alia Sheikh, Peter Mills, Rebecca Stagg, Michael Evans

        2022 Aug

        White Paper WHP 402 Download Aug 2022

        Abstract

        In this paper, we describe the rationale for, and the process and outcomes of, a shoot that BBC R&D carried out in 2021 to create the Old School dataset. The main use case for the dataset is to further research into AI automated editing systems. We introduce our way of thinking about these systems and explain why we believe a widely available dataset is necessary if research into these systems is to reach its potential. We then explain why a new shoot was necessary to generate such material.

        We proceed to give an overview of the shootâ€™s creative process. We do this with a particular focus on explaining those aspects of the shoot which differed from conventional TV production in order to produce a dataset with the desired technical and editorial characteristics. Different sections are provided that cover the planning and pre-production stage, the shoot itself, and the post-shoot work to process the material and generate metadata.

        We finish with a summary of the material and metadata that have been included in the asset bundle for the dataset that will be shared with collaborators, and an overview of the process by which we determined what to include in this bundle.

        An Appendix to the document includes the full credits for the shoot; the authors offer their sincere thanks to all those who are listed therein.
